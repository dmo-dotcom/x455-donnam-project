About Glossier

Before we launched Glossier in 2014, we started as Into The Gloss. A beauty website and community devoted to sharing real information with real people, about really amazing products. It's where we realized that beauty shouldn't be built in a boardroom—it should be built by you. This ethos gave birth to our philosophy: Skin First. Makeup Second™. We prioritize skincare because we believe that healthy skin is the best foundation. We believe in thoughtful design and cultivating conversations. It's at the core of everything we do—the starting point of all our makeup, skincare, bodycare, and fragrance products. At Glossier, beauty is about celebrating freedom of expression, individuality , and having fun. Our Skin First. Makeup Second™ approach ensures that every product supports your skin's health, allowing your makeup to enhance rather than mask your natural beauty.

Meet Our Founder


Emily Weiss
Founder of Glossier

Emily Weiss is the visionary founder and of Glossier. With a background in fashion and beauty journalism, Emily launched Glossier in 2014 as a digital-first brand that prioritizes the voices and desires of its community of customers. Under Emily's leadership, Glossier has grown into a global phenomenon, renowned for its minimalist aesthetic and cult-favorite products that celebrate natural beauty. Emily's journey from beauty editor to entrepreneur has been marked by a commitment to authenticity and a deep understanding of what consumers truly want from their beauty products. Beyond Glossier, Emily is a champion of women in business and entrepreneurship, inspiring a new generation of leaders through her insights on building a brand in the digital age. Her influence extends far beyond the beauty industry, making her a prominent figure in the realm of e-commerce and direct-to-consumer marketing.
